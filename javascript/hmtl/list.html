Dereks Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package instant yeast
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 egg

For the filling:

  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened

Instructions

  1. Heat the milk in a small saucepan until it bubbles, then remove from heat. Mix in butter; stir until melted. Let cool until lukewarm.

      2. Spread dough with butter/sugar mixture.
      3. Roll up dough and pinch seam to seal.
      4. Cut into 12 equal size rolls and place cut side up in 12 lightly greased muffin cups.
      5. Cover and let rise until doubled, about 30 minutes.
